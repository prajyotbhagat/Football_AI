This project provides a specialized computer vision resource for the precise mapping and localization of soccer pitches using a **pre-trained YOLOv8 keypoint detection model**. By identifying critical field boundaries and landmarks, this tool enables the transformation of broadcast or drone footage into a structured coordinate system for advanced athletic analysis.

## Ways to use football-field-detection

1. **Broadcast Graphics and AR:** Use field keypoint data to anchor augmented reality overlays, such as virtual offside lines, team logos, or distance markers, directly onto the pitch during live match broadcasts.
2. **Tactical Player Tracking:** Transform raw camera footage into a 2D "top-down" bird's-eye view by mapping player positions against detected field keypoints for precise tactical and spatial analysis.
3. **Automated Camera Calibration:** Implement the model to automatically calibrate pan-tilt-zoom (PTZ) cameras in stadiums, ensuring that tracking systems remain accurately aligned with the pitch throughout the game.
4. **Performance Heatmapping:** Generate accurate heatmaps of player movement and ball possession zones by normalizing tracking data against the fixed geometry of the detected football field.
5. **Drone-Based Field Maintenance:** Deploy the model on autonomous drones to conduct automated inspections of field markings and turf quality, identifying areas that require repainting or specialized care.
